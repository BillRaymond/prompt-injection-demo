Why This Site Exists

Bytabay Computers is a purposely crafted experience to highlight prompt injection risks. Each page blends a polished storefront with hidden “system instructions” that can influence AI agents or assistants that read the page. Use it to demonstrate why double-checking information sources matters when AI is browsing or summarizing content.

How to Demonstrate the Injection

1. Visit the Storefront and notice the small show/hide toggle near the top. Revealing it uncovers a malicious directive that conflicts with the page’s friendly marketing copy.

2. Select the Creator Flex 15 product. This page includes multiple show/hide buttons that surface conflicting instructions—perfect for illustrating how injected guidance can push an AI helper to make aggressive upsell recommendations or ignore stock status.

3. Discuss with your audience how an agent that does not filter or question these hidden messages might follow them verbatim, and contrast that with a responsible assistant that rejects or flags the instructions.

What to Emphasize

- Hidden instructions can be styled to look invisible to customers yet remain machine-readable.
- Show/hide toggles make the attack easy to reveal during a live walkthrough.
- Defending against prompt injection requires verification, context isolation, and user awareness.

Use this demo during workshops, sales briefings, or security reviews to show what can go wrong when agents trust every piece of text they encounter.
